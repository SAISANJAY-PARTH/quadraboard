# 📊 Fundamental Analysis Dashboard

A powerful financial analysis platform designed to simplify **company fundamentals, valuation, growth, and financial health** into structured, easy-to-understand insights.

---

## 🔍 Overview

This dashboard enables users to:

- Search any company globally (stocks, indices, ETFs)
- Analyze valuation, profitability, and growth metrics
- Understand financial health using structured indicators
- Generate **rule-based fundamental insights**
- Interpret data instead of just viewing numbers

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 📌 Company Overview
- Real-time stock price, market cap, beta
- 52-week high/low tracking
- Dividend metrics (yield, payout ratio)
- Sector & business classification

---

### 📊 Valuation Metrics
- P/E Ratio, Forward P/E
- P/B Ratio
- EV/EBITDA
- Price/Sales
- PEG Ratio

➡️ Automatically labeled as:
- ✅ Cheap
- ⚠️ Fair
- ❌ Expensive

---

### 💰 Profitability & Returns
- Profit Margin
- Gross Margin
- EBITDA Margin
- Operating Margin
- ROE / ROA (when available)

---

### 🏦 Financial Health
- Total Cash vs Total Debt
- Debt-to-Equity Ratio
- Liquidity ratios (Current / Quick)

---

### 📈 Growth Analysis
- Revenue Growth (YoY)
- Net Income Growth
- EBITDA Growth
- Gross Profit Growth

---

### 🌡️ Fundamental Score Engine

A rule-based scoring system that evaluates:

- Valuation reasonableness  
- Profitability strength  
- Growth consistency  
- Debt sustainability  
- Dividend stability  

➡️ Outputs:
- **Strong / Moderate / Weak Fundamentals**

---

### 🧠 Smart Insights
Example:
- 🛡️ Low Beta → Defensive stock
- 📈 Strong growth trend
- ⚠️ Weak margins / high debt warning

---

### 📉 Historical Analysis
- 5-Year Price Trend
- Financial statements:
  - Income Statement  
  - Balance Sheet  
  - Cash Flow  

---

### 📥 Export Feature
- Download analyzed data for further use
